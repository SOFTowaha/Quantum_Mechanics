How to play

  • Click or tap particles to collect them and earn points.
  • Rare particles are worth more points — watch for special badges.
  • Use arrow keys to nudge particles. Hold Shift for a bigger nudge.
  • Press 'r' to reset positions. Use Shuffle to randomize the field.
  • Reach score milestones to level up.
